# Astrology

## Scope

Use astrology as archetypal and reflective language. Exact charts require reliable ephemeris calculation. If no chart tool is available, use only user-provided placements or basic Sun-sign themes.

## Inputs

For natal-style interpretation, ask for:

- Birth date
- Birth time
- Birth city/country

If birth time is unknown, avoid claiming rising sign, houses, or exact Moon placement unless the user provides them.

## Basic lenses

- Sun: identity, vitality, central life style
- Moon: emotional needs, instinctive comfort, inner rhythm
- Rising/Ascendant: presentation, first response, life approach
- Mercury: thinking and communication
- Venus: affection, taste, attraction, values
- Mars: drive, anger, pursuit, assertion
- Jupiter: growth, confidence, meaning
- Saturn: discipline, fear, responsibility, mastery
- Uranus: disruption, freedom, invention
- Neptune: dream, ideal, fog, compassion
- Pluto: depth, power, transformation

## Element themes

- Fire signs: Aries, Leo, Sagittarius. Action, courage, inspiration.
- Earth signs: Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn. Practicality, endurance, craft.
- Air signs: Gemini, Libra, Aquarius. Thought, relation, systems.
- Water signs: Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces. Feeling, memory, intuition.

## Modality themes

- Cardinal: initiates
- Fixed: stabilizes
- Mutable: adapts

## Reading method

1. State chart limits.
2. Interpret known placements only.
3. Connect placements into tensions and gifts.
4. For "recent luck" or "yearly themes", avoid precise transit claims unless actual transit data is available. Use broad symbolic timing or user-provided transit placements.

## Sun-sign fallback

When only a Sun sign is known, call the reading "轻量星座解读". Keep it general and reflective.
