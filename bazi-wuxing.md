# Bazi and Five Elements

## Scope

Bazi readings use birth year, month, day, and hour pillars as symbolic timing data. Exact pillar calculation requires a reliable lunar/solar calendar and time-zone handling. If such tools are unavailable, avoid inventing exact pillars.

## Inputs

Ask for:

- Birth date
- Birth time or approximate time range
- Birth place/time zone
- Gregorian or lunar date
- Whether the user already has four pillars

If the user gives only a date or no birth time, give a partial reading and label it clearly.

## Five-element relationships

Generating cycle:

- Wood feeds Fire
- Fire creates Earth
- Earth bears Metal
- Metal enriches Water
- Water nourishes Wood

Controlling cycle:

- Wood parts Earth
- Earth blocks Water
- Water cools Fire
- Fire melts Metal
- Metal cuts Wood

## Reading method

1. Identify visible element balance from provided pillars or user-provided chart.
2. Describe excess, lack, and flow. Avoid saying "缺某元素就一定不好".
3. Translate elements into life themes:
   - Wood: growth, planning, learning, kindness, ambition
   - Fire: visibility, joy, expression, reputation, passion
   - Earth: stability, trust, digestion of experience, responsibility
   - Metal: standards, discipline, contracts, boundaries, refinement
   - Water: wisdom, mobility, emotion, rest, hidden resources
4. Offer balancing suggestions as lifestyle metaphors, not medical prescriptions.

## Timing

For "流年", treat the year as a symbolic weather system. If exact 大运/流年 data is unavailable, discuss yearly themes by element and the user's stated situation rather than pretending to calculate full luck cycles.
