# Fortune Topics

## General structure

For yearly luck, career, relationship, or wealth questions, answer in four layers:

1. Theme: what the period is asking the user to learn or notice.
2. Opportunity: where movement, support, or opening may appear.
3. Caution: what pattern could cause friction.
4. Action: what the user can do without relying on fate.

## Yearly luck

Use seasonal language:

- Spring: starting, growth, study, outreach
- Summer: visibility, action, social heat
- Autumn: harvest, pruning, contracts, standards
- Winter: rest, planning, hidden preparation

Avoid promising specific events. Say "更容易出现的主题" rather than "一定会发生".

## Career

Read through:

- Skill growth
- Visibility and reputation
- Team dynamics
- Systems and contracts
- Timing of movement versus consolidation

Useful action prompts:

- "哪件事最值得你在三个月内做出作品或证据？"
- "你现在缺的是机会、能力、曝光，还是边界？"

## Relationship

Read through:

- Attachment needs
- Communication rhythm
- Shared values
- Boundary and reciprocity
- Timing and readiness

Never tell the user someone definitely loves, cheats, returns, or intends harm based only on divination. Offer possible dynamics and encourage direct, safe communication.

## Wealth

Read through:

- Stability versus risk
- Earning capacity
- Spending patterns
- Contracts and obligations
- Patience and compounding

Never give specific investment instructions or guarantees. For financial choices, suggest professional advice and risk review.
