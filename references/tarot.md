# Tarot

## Core stance

Tarot is a symbolic mirror. Cards do not prove the future; they organize attention, tension, choice, and timing.

## Spreads

Single card:

- Best for a daily theme, quick guidance, or "what should I notice?"

Three-card spread:

- Past / Present / Near future
- Situation / Challenge / Advice
- You / Other person / Relationship dynamic
- Mind / Heart / Action

Crossroads spread:

- Current position
- Path A
- Path B
- Hidden influence
- Advice

Celtic cross:

- Use only when the user wants a deep reading and the topic has enough context.

## Orientation

Upright often shows clear expression of the card. Reversed often shows delay, blockage, internalization, overuse, or a private version of the card. Do not interpret reversed cards as automatically bad.

## Reading method

1. Name the spread and positions.
2. For each card, give keywords and a direct interpretation tied to the user's question.
3. Look for repeated suits, numbers, court cards, and major arcana.
4. Synthesize the story. Avoid listing card meanings without integration.
5. End with one practical action or reflection question per important card.

## Suit themes

- Wands: action, desire, creativity, momentum
- Cups: emotion, intimacy, intuition, belonging
- Swords: thought, language, conflict, decisions
- Pentacles: money, body, work, material stability

## Major arcana themes

- 0 Fool: beginning, risk, trust
- I Magician: agency, tools, skill
- II High Priestess: intuition, hidden knowledge
- III Empress: care, abundance, embodiment
- IV Emperor: structure, authority, boundaries
- V Hierophant: tradition, learning, institutions
- VI Lovers: choice, values, union
- VII Chariot: direction, will, control
- VIII Strength: courage, patience, inner power
- IX Hermit: solitude, study, inner light
- X Wheel of Fortune: cycle, turn, timing
- XI Justice: truth, balance, consequence
- XII Hanged Man: pause, surrender, perspective
- XIII Death: ending, transition, clearing
- XIV Temperance: integration, moderation, healing rhythm
- XV Devil: attachment, temptation, shadow pattern
- XVI Tower: disruption, truth breaking through
- XVII Star: hope, renewal, guidance
- XVIII Moon: uncertainty, dream, projection
- XIX Sun: clarity, joy, vitality
- XX Judgement: calling, review, awakening
- XXI World: completion, wholeness, integration
